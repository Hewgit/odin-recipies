Potatoey Soup

Potatoes In A Soup

It's a soup that has potatoes in it.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ white onion, chopped
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 4 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 tablespoon dried, minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons chicken soup base
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 5 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices crisp cooked bacon, crumbled

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Cook potatoes
  3. Cook everything
  4. Remove bay leaf
  5. Puree soup in food processor or feed to bird to regurgitate
  6. Stir in cheese and bacon until melted
  7. Eat
